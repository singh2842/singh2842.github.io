Life of a Starfish

Discover the amazing world beneath the sea

About Starfish

Starfish are marine animals known for their unique star-shaped body. They have the ability to regenerate lost arms, which makes them fascinating creatures in the ocean world.

  • Found in oceans all over the world
  • Have a water vascular system
  • Feed on mollusks like clams and oysters

Interesting Facts

Starfish do not have a brain or blood. Instead, they use seawater to pump nutrients through their body. Their outer skin is often rough and spiny for protection.

  • Can regenerate lost arms
  • Some can live up to 35 years
  • Come in many colors: red, orange, blue, purple
