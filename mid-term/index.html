Life of a Starfish

Starfish are fascinating marine animals that can regenerate lost arms. They are found in oceans all over the world. Starfish play an important role in the marine ecosystem by helping control the population of other marine organisms. They move slowly, have no brain, and yet exhibit amazing survival skills.

Some species of starfish can live up to 35 years, and their unique ability to regenerate arms makes them a symbol of resilience. Studying starfish helps scientists understand more about marine biology and regenerative medicine.

Interesting Facts About Starfish

  • Starfish have no brain or blood.
  • They can regenerate lost arms.
  • Some species can live up to 35 years.
  • They have a water vascular system to move and feed.
